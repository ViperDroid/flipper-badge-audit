# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want this project to be a welcoming, harassment-free space for everyone,
regardless of experience level or background.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent; give and accept feedback gracefully.
- Keep discussions focused and on-topic.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, insults, or discriminatory comments.
- Sharing others' private information without consent.
- Using this tool, or discussions here, to encourage unauthorized access.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, commits, or contributions that violate this
policy, and may block repeat offenders. Report concerns to the maintainer.
